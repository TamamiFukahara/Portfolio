Project information

  • Category: Grayscale & Prototype
  • Proto Persona: a 38 year-old cafe owner who is seeking information on local apples for her new menu
  • Theme & Concept: A prototype introducing apple varieties
  • Production Period: 5 weeks
  • Project date: October-November, 2022
  • Tools: Adobe Illustrator & XD

iPhone App

It was my first series of processes from hand-drawn wire frames to creating grayscale, design camps using Adobe XD and logo design using Illustrator.

We tried to understand the customer's requirements and what this application was for, and to come up with a design that was faithful to those requirements.

Making a site map and dividing each group by color helped understand the overall app structure.
