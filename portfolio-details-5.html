Project information

  • Category: Graphic Design
  • Theme & Concept: Representing the four seasons in text and color.
  • Production Period: 1 week
  • Project date: November, 2022
  • Tools: Adobe Illustrator

Typography

I depicted the four seasons in text and colors.

First, I divided the art board into four sections, and colored each section to represent one of the four seasons. Next, I picked a font that I imagined from the four seasons.

I used the default fonts included in Adobe Illustrator, but in the future, I would like to research fonts published in various resources and work on typography design as well.
